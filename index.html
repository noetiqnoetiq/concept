Who We Are

Noetiq is a fashion-focused market intelligence platform that goes beyond ordinary analytics. We blend industry expertise with advanced AI, enhanced by human sensitivity, to uncover insights that simply don't exist elsewhere in the market.

Why We're Different

Our proprietary methods dive deep into product attributes (like silhouettes, fabrics, product details, trends and price architecture in a granularity no one else does), revealing white-space opportunities and untapped potential for fashion brands. We don't just present insights—we translate them into actionable strategies, tailored to each brand's specific needs, goals, and key objectives. Our insights and strategies align with their unique business priorities (whether increasing margin, scaling operations, introducing new categories, capturing market share, or elevating brand positioning) then dovetail with the market insights found among competitor landscape that drive real results.

Our USP

  1. Fashion-First Approach – Built by fashion and tech experts combined to merge the future of fashion, who understand the real drivers of brand growth.
  2. AI + Human Expertise – Powerful automation plus expert curation for accurate, business-savvy insights. While we leverage AI technology, human expertise and connection remain the foundation of our business—this human element is what truly sets us apart.
  3. Beyond Dashboards – We provide clear, data-driven recommendations that elevate your product lines and pricing strategies allowing you to see what others don't.

Our Proprietary Methodologies
